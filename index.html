Our Story

Over 10 Years on the Water

David has spent the past decade running kayak fishing charters through his business, South Florida Uncharted, specializing in Hobie kayaks. After years of experience on the water, he began designing innovative products specifically for Hobie kayaks, making them easier to use and even better to fish from.

Every product is built from real-world experience to help you fish smarter, stay more comfortable, and land more catches.
